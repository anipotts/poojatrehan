# Admin Guide - Pooja Trehan Portfolio

Welcome to your portfolio admin panel! This guide will help you manage and update your portfolio with ease.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Admin Panel

1. Navigate to **poojatrehan.vercel.app/admin** (or your custom domain/admin)
2. Enter your username and password
3. Click "Sign In"

### Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, you'll see the admin dashboard with these sections:

- **Profile** - Edit your personal information and hero section
- **Experience** - Manage your work experience entries
- **Education** - Update your education history
- **Skills** - Add or remove skills
- **Theme** - Customize colors and appearance

The top bar shows:
- **Preview** button - See your changes before publishing
- **Publish** button - Make your changes live
- **Logout** icon - Sign out of the admin panel

---

## Editing Your Profile

### Personal Information

1. Click the **Profile** tab
2. Update any of these fields:
   - **Full Name** - Your display name
   - **Title** - Your professional title (e.g., "Economics Student")
   - **Email** - Your contact email
   - **Location** - Where you're based
   - **Profile Description** - A brief introduction

### Uploading a Profile Photo

1. In the Profile tab, find the **Profile Image** section
2. Click **Upload Image**
3. Select a photo from your computer (PNG or JPG, max 5MB)
4. Wait for the upload to complete
5. Your new photo will appear immediately

**Tips:**
- Use a professional, well-lit headshot
- Square or portrait orientation works best
- File should be clear and high-quality

### Hero Section

The hero section is the first thing visitors see. Update:
- **Status Badge** - Short current status (e.g., "Senior Economics student at NYU")
- **Hero Title** - Main headline (keep it impactful!)
- **Hero Subtitle** - Supporting description

**Don't forget to click "Save Changes" at the bottom!**

## Managing Experience

### Adding a New Experience

1. Click the **Experience** tab
2. Click **Add Experience** (green button)
3. Fill in all fields:
   - **Company** - Where you worked
   - **Role** - Your job title
   - **Type** - Internship, Full-time, Part-time, etc.
   - **Location** - Where the job was based
   - **Start Date** - When you started (e.g., "May 2024")
   - **End Date** - When you finished (e.g., "Aug 2024")
   - **Key Achievements** - Click "Add Bullet" to describe what you did
4. Click **Save**

### Editing Experience

1. Find the experience entry you want to edit
2. Click the **Edit** button
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save**

### Deleting Experience

1. Find the experience entry you want to remove
2. Click the trash icon (üóëÔ∏è)

**Pro Tip:** Write achievements in past tense and focus on results!

## Managing Skills

### Adding Skills

1. Click the **Skills** tab
2. Type a skill name in the input field
3. Press **Enter** or click the **+** button
4. The skill appears as a badge below

### Removing Skills

1. Hover over any skill badge
2. Click the **X** that appears
3. The skill is removed immediately

**Best Practices:**
- Keep skills relevant to your career goals
- List 5-10 most important skills
- Be specific (e.g., "Financial Modeling" instead of just "Finance")

## Customizing Your Theme

### Changing Colors

1. Click the **Theme** tab
2. Click on a color picker (Primary or Accent)
3. Choose your desired color
4. See a live preview below
5. Click **Save Theme**

**Color Tips:**
- **Primary Color** - Used for buttons and links
- **Accent Color** - Used for highlights
- Keep colors professional and ensure good contrast

### Resetting Theme

If you want to go back to the default colors, click **Reset to Default**.

## Preview & Publish Workflow

### Preview Your Changes

**IMPORTANT:** Changes are saved as a **draft** and won't appear on your live site until you publish.

1. After making changes, click **Preview** in the top bar
2. A modal will show how your portfolio will look
3. Review everything carefully
4. Close the preview when done

### Publishing Changes

1. When you're happy with your changes, click **Publish**
2. A success message will appear
3. Your live portfolio is now updated!
4. Visit your public URL to verify

**Safety Note:** Always preview before publishing to catch any mistakes!

## Best Practices

### Content Writing

- **Be concise** - Keep descriptions clear and to the point
- **Use action verbs** - Start bullets with strong verbs (Built, Managed, Led, etc.)
- **Quantify when possible** - Include numbers and results
- **Proofread** - Check for typos before publishing

### Image Guidelines

- **Professional photos only** - No selfies or casual photos
- **Good lighting** - Make sure your face is well-lit
- **Clean background** - Avoid busy or distracting backgrounds
- **High resolution** - Use clear, sharp images

### Regular Updates

- Update experience as you complete internships
- Add new skills as you learn them
- Keep your profile description current
- Refresh your photo every 1-2 years

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?
- Check your username and password for typos
- Make sure Caps Lock is off
- Contact Ani if you've forgotten your password

### Image Won't Upload?
- Check file size (must be under 5MB)
- Make sure it's a PNG or JPG file
- Try a different browser if issues persist

### Changes Not Showing?
- Did you click "Save Changes" or "Save"?
- Did you click "Publish" after saving?
- Try refreshing your browser (Cmd+R or Ctrl+R)
- Clear your browser cache

### Preview Not Working?
- Make sure you've saved changes first
- Try logging out and back in
- Contact Ani if the problem persists
